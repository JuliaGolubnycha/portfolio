About me

Front-end developer from Kyiv, Ukraine

Hey there! I'm Yuliia, a passionate programmer with a dream as big as the universe itself. Ever since I was a kid, I've been gazing at the stars, dreaming of becoming an astronomer and unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos. But as I grew older, I realized that the path to exploring the universe has evolved. Instead of peering through telescopes, today's astronomers crunch numbers and write complex algorithms. That's when I decided to embark on a journey into the world of programming. But I'm not just your average coder; I'm on a mission to merge my love for space with my programming skills to propel humanity to the stars. Imagine launching spacecraft, writing code that manages every aspect of the journey, from navigation to life support systems and scientific experiments. That's the kind of challenge that gets me excited! But I don't stop there. I'm also passionate about building the spacecraft themselves, using my programming prowess to design efficient and safe vessels that will carry future generations of astronauts to new frontiers. And let's not forget about the team. While I may not have a stellar crew yet, I'm eager to assemble a dream team of like-minded individuals who share my vision of exploring the cosmos. Together, we'll push the boundaries of what's possible and inspire the world with our achievements. So if you're looking for a programmer who's not afraid to aim for the stars (literally), then look no further. Let's connect and make the impossible possible.
